Pediatric & Congenital Electrophysiology Society (PACES)

Welcome to the online home of the Pediatric & Congenital Electrophysiology Society, an international group of physicians and allied professionals dedicated to improving the care of children and young adults with cardiac rhythm disturbances.

A nonprofit organization, the group's primary mission is to foster high-quality collaborative research and exchange of ideas on arrhythmia topics that are particularly relevant to infants and children, or patients of any age with congenital heart disease.
